Tsuki

☰

Experience delectable Japanese food in a traditional dining setting. Zagat rated for over 15 years, the skill of our chefs' years of experience and training is reflected in every dish. Tsuki is a must-have for all Japanese cuisine enthusiasts.

For private bookings, Tsuki offers private rooms to accommodate parties, corporate events, and private dining. Please make a reservation at 908-895-0450 and we will be happy to serve you. Tsuki is a BYOB resturant.
