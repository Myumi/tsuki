About Tsuki

いらっしゃいませ!

Experience delectable Japanese food in a traditional dining setting. Zagat rated for over 15 years, the skill of our chefs' years of experience and training is reflected in every dish. Tsuki is a must for all sushi and sashimi lovers!

For private bookings, Tsuki offers private rooms to accomidate parties, corporate events, and private dining. Please make a reservation at 908-895-0450 and we will be happy to serve you.
